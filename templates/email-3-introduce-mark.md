**Word target:** ~100 words
**Leads required:** 1 (with named contact)

```
Subject: Spotted another one for you -- [Project Name], [Town]

Hi [Name],

Another one that looks relevant:

[Project Name], [Town] -- [why relevant, using package language]. The person to speak to is [Contact Name] ([Contact Role]) at [Contact Company] -- [email / phone if available].

If that sounds useful, happy to jump on a quick call and show you how it works.

Thanks,
Andy
```
