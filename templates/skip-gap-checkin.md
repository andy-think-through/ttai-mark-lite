# Skip-Gap Check-In

**Purpose:** Keep relationship warm when no matching leads available for 2+ consecutive weeks.
**When to use:** Prospect skipped 2+ consecutive weeks due to no matching leads (trade + size + explicit confidence).

## Template

```
Subject: Keeping an eye out for you

Hi [Name],

Haven't had anything come through that's a good fit for [trade] work this week, but I'm keeping an eye out. I'll drop you a line when I spot something.

Thanks,
Andy
```

## Rules

- Does NOT count as an email in the 4-email sequence. Do not increment Last Email # Drafted.
- Log in Outreach_Log with Email # = "Check-in" and Status = "Drafted".
- Maximum ONE check-in per prospect across the entire sequence.
- If skipped again after check-in, wait silently until leads are available.
- Do NOT send if prospect was only skipped for 1 week.
